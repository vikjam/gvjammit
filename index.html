Event Details

We will be getting married in a small, immediate-family-only ceremony on July 14, 2017.

We would love for you to join us on Saturday, July 15th, to celebrate! We will be hosting a party on July 15th from 6pm-9pm at

Ball Square Bowling Alley
662 Boston Ave
Medford, MA 02155

The party will be casual -- feel free to dress up as much or as a little as you like. Children are welcome! We will be playing candlepin bowling, a New England variation. Dinner and drinks will be available throughout the evening.

There is on-street parking on Boston Ave and Broadway (cross-street). The bowling alley is 0.7 miles from the Davis Square station on the Red Line (about a 15 minute walk). It can also be easily accessed via your preferred on-demand transportation service.

Accommodations

Given how compact Greater Boston is, hotels and other accommodations located in Boston, Cambridge, or Somerville would be convenient to get to the Ball Square Bowling Alley as well as any sights in and around Boston.

The party venue is located on the Red Line of the MBTA, which is easily accessed through all the other subway lines and the commuter rail.


There are plenty of Airbnb options in the area. We are happy to answer any questions about specific neighborhoods or cities. Airbnb also has a useful city guide.

Activities and Attractions

Summer in Boston and New England is lovely. Here’s some of our favorite warm-weather outings.

  • Enjoy an afternoon on the Freedom Trail or the Black Heritage Trail
  • Take a trip to the Boston Harbor Islands
  • Visit the Museum of Fine Arts or the Institute of Contemporary Art
  • Visit the John F. Kennedy Presidential Library & Museum
  • Take in a game at Fenway
  • Take a day-trip to Salem or Rockport/Gloucester
  • Head to World's Ends in Hingham for a nice stroll and view of Boston
